Join Our School

We welcome applications from students who are eager to learn and grow. Our admission process is designed to be simple and straightforward.

Admission Process

1
Submit Application

Fill out the online application form with all required information about the student and parent/guardian.

2
Document Submission

Upload or submit required documents including previous academic records, birth certificate, and passport photos.

3
Assessment/Interview

Attend a brief assessment or interview session. This helps us understand the student's academic level and needs.

4
Admission Decision

Receive your admission decision within 5-7 working days. Successful applicants will receive an admission letter.

5
Complete Enrollment

Pay the required fees and complete the enrollment process. Welcome to our school family!
